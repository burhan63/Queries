-- Health Policy Activation and De-Activation --
Agar apkai pass health mai sy request aye kai policy deactivate karni hai
tw ins_assorted mai aik column hai cancel_by us mai jakar bus SYS karna hai.
Query:
select iz.cancel_by from Ins_Assorted iz
where iz.assorted_string like '%3371-12-2024%' and iz.policy_type_code = '76'
for update

-- Agar De-Activate kani hai tw cancel_by mai SYS karna hai 
-- Agar de-activated policy ko activate karna hai tw cancel_by mai sy SYS 
remove karna hai.
